III · Phase Space · Phase 2

Condensation,
& Cloud Formation.

As vapour rises, the air around it cools. At the dew point, it can hold no more — droplets form around dust, pollen, salt. A cloud is millions of these, suspended.

dew point
the temperature at which a parcel of air
becomes saturated. higher altitude →
colder air → easier to reach saturation.
