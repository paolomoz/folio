V · Loop · Why It Matters

The cycle holds. — for now.

Four stations, on repeat, for four billion years. The same molecules that fell as rain on Pangaea are in the rivers we drink from today.

But of all the water on Earth, almost none is ours to drink. The rest is salt, ice, or out of reach.
