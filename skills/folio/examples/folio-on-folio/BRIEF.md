## Notes

This is the meta-example: folio describing itself, using its own patterns, palette discipline, and four-phase flow. It demonstrates the skill end-to-end and serves as a regression test — if any future change breaks the example, the change is suspect.

The deck deliberately uses the **default specimen palette** (cyan/violet on dark) rather than picking from `palettes.json`, because the point is to demonstrate the chrome and patterns, not to showcase one palette in particular. The `palettes-demo/` gallery does that job better.
