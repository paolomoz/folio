IV · Versus · Phase 3

Precipitation, & Collection.

Rain or snow falls. At the soil, every drop chooses one of two paths — and the cycle keeps both.

runoff. — above the soil

what cannot soak in flows downhill — across rooftops, fields, asphalt — gathering into streams and rivers on its way back to the sea.

→ to rivers, lakes, the ocean
↤ soil surface

infiltration. — below the soil

the rest seeps down through soil and rock — pooling in aquifers, surfacing in springs, held in the dark for years, sometimes millennia, before joining the cycle again.

↓ aquifers · springs · wells
